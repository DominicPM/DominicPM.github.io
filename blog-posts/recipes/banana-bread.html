Banana bread

Click here to go back.

Ingredients

  • 450 grams plain white flour,
  • 175 grams caster sugar,
  • 100 grams softened unsalted butter,
  • 25 grams soft brown sugar,
  • Four medium eggs,
  • Four very ripe bananas,
  • Four tablespoons whole milk,
  • Two teaspoons baking powder,
  • One teaspoon vanilla essence,
  • One teaspoon ground nutmeg,
  • A pinch of salt.

Method

Pre-heat a fan oven to 180 degrees Celsius and grease a large loaf tin. Roughly mash the bananas and then add all ingredients to a stand-mixer bowl and beat on a medium speed for two-to-three minutes. Empty into the pre-greased loaf tin and bake for between 55 minutes and one hour until risen, golden brown, and a skewer comes out clean when the centre of the loaf is skewered.
